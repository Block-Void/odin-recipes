Lasagna

Description

Classic Lasagna when layers of cheesy lasagna come together flawlessly, that's amore! Pile on beefy marinara sauce, delicious noodles, and a blend of cheeses, then kick back and let this lasagna do the bakin'. This Italian dinner is easy to whip up and even easier to call your go-to comfort food.

Ingredients

for 6 servings

  • nonstick cooking spray, for greasing
  • 1 lb ground beef(455 g), cooked and drained of fat
  • 4 cups marinara sauce (920 g)
  • 4 cups of whole milk ricotta cheese ()
  • 2 cups grated parmesan cheese (220 g)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 15 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (300 g)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Fahreinheit (180 Celsius). Grease a 9 x 13-inch (22 x 33 cm) baking dish lightly with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large bowl,mix together the ground beef and marinara sauce.
  3. In a seperate large bowl, combine the ricotta, Parmesan, parsley, basil, salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce over the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  5. Top with 5 cooked lasagna noodles, overlapping as needed to completely cover the sauce.
  6. Spread 2 cups of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  7. Top with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese. Repeat with the remaining meat sauce, noodles , ricotta, and mozzarella to make 2 more layers.
  8. Bake the lasagna for 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbling up through the layers.
  9. Turn the broiler to high and broil for 1-2 minutes, until the top layer of cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 30-45 minutes>, then serve.
  11. And voila the dish is done.
